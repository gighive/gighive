# Trust & Safety Policy

## Purpose

GigHive exists to help users preserve, organize, and responsibly share
legitimate media collections. Trust & Safety protects users, creators,
and the platform while supporting that mission.

## Guiding Principles

-   Design for good-faith users.
-   Build safeguards against abuse.
-   Be transparent about moderation decisions.
-   Respect creators and rights holders.
-   Separate policy decisions from technical implementation.

## Prohibited Content

The platform is not intended to host or facilitate:

-   Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
-   Non-Consensual Intimate Imagery (NCII)
-   Copyright infringement or piracy
-   Malware or malicious files
-   Fraud, impersonation, or deceptive content
-   Other illegal content under applicable law

The platform may also prohibit pornography or sexually explicit content
as a product policy to keep GigHive focused on its intended purpose.

## Enforcement

Layered safeguards include:

-   Account requirements
-   User attestations
-   Metadata and provenance preservation
-   Rights and ownership indicators
-   Reporting tools
-   Human review where appropriate
-   Takedown and appeal processes
-   Audit logging

## Repeat Violations

Repeated or egregious violations may result in content removal, account
restrictions, or account termination, consistent with applicable law and
platform policy.

## Relationship to the Architecture

This policy defines **what** GigHive permits.

The Media Integrity Architecture defines **how** GigHive implements
those decisions.
