# GigHive iPhone App

GigHive includes a companion iPhone app that makes it easy for fans, guests, and contributors to upload music and video to a GigHive server.

The app is designed to work alongside a self-hosted GigHive installation and gives users a simple mobile upload experience for event media.

- App Store: [GigHive - Upload Music, Video](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/gighive-upload-music-video/id6753146513)
- Use it together with your GigHive server for mobile uploads
